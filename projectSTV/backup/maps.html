Maps & Variants

Explore the realms of Dead by Daylight — from eerie forests to sinister institutions, each map holds secrets and challenges.

Autohaven Wreckers

A rundown junkyard filled with broken vehicles and debris. Perfect for close-quarter chases and ambushes.

Lery’s Memorial Institute

A haunting abandoned mental hospital. Tight corridors and multi-level floors create intense chase scenarios.

MacMillan Estate

A sprawling plantation with dense woods and hidden structures. Survivors must stay aware of long sightlines.

The Pale Rose

A misty carnival area with traps and obstacles. Survivors can use distractions to outwit killers.

Coldwind Farm

A rural map with open fields and barn structures. Generators are often spread far apart, testing map awareness.
